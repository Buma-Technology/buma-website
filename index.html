Questions we hear before every discovery call.

What does an AI automation project actually look like?+

It depends on the workflow. Common builds: an agent that parses vendor invoices and posts them as NetSuite vendor bills, a model that classifies and routes inbound order emails, or an exception-resolution agent that fixes routine order issues before they hit a human queue. Every AI build runs inside the same iPaaS infrastructure as our integrations — monitored, retried, logged.

How much does a typical engagement cost?+

Every engagement is tailored — single integration builds through multi-system orchestration with AI agents. You get a number and a timeline before any contract is signed.

Do you only work with Celigo and NetSuite?+

Celigo and NetSuite are where we ship fastest because that's where our deepest expertise lives. We also build on custom integrations when a project requires it. For ERPs other than NetSuite, we integrate through iPaaS rather than re-implementing the ERP itself. Tell us your stack, we'll tell you the right path.

What if we already have a Celigo instance running?+

Many of our engagements are audit-and-rebuild on existing Celigo deployments — flows built by previous partners that don't error-handle, don't document, or don't scale. We can scope a fixed-fee audit before any rebuild work.

What happens after go-live?+

Every engagement ends with monitoring active, runbooks delivered, and your team trained. Most clients stay on a lightweight retainer for new platforms, new trading partners, or new AI agents — but it's optional. If you want it in-house, the documentation supports that handoff. We don't hold integrations hostage.

How quickly can you start?+

Signed contract to discovery kickoff is typically 1–2 weeks. Discovery to go-live runs a structured timeline for a focused build, longer for multi-system orchestration. You get a calendar at scoping.
